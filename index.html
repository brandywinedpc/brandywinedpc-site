How Direct Primary Care Works

Direct Primary Care (DPC) is a membership-based healthcare model that puts the doctor-patient relationship first. Instead of dealing with insurance for routine care, members pay a simple monthly fee that covers:

  • All office visits and consultations
  • Basic procedures and screenings
  • Care coordination and referrals
  • Direct access to your physician
  • Wholesale pricing on medications when available

Note: DPC memberships work alongside catastrophic insurance or health sharing plans for emergencies and specialist care.
