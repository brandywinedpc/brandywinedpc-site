Professional Experience

Before launching Brandywine Direct Primary Care, Dr. Katz gained valuable experience at ChristianaCare Primary Care at Limestone, one of Delaware's leading healthcare systems. This experience reinforced her belief that the traditional healthcare model often doesn't allow physicians enough time with their patients.

As a member of the American Academy of Family Physicians (AAFP), Dr. Katz stays current with the latest advances in family medicine and primary care delivery models. Her board certification in family medicine demonstrates her commitment to maintaining the highest standards of medical knowledge and patient care.

Why Direct Primary Care?

Dr. Katz chose to establish a Direct Primary Care practice because she believes healthcare should be personal, accessible, and affordable. The DPC model allows her to limit her patient panel, ensuring each patient receives the time, attention, and personalized care they deserve.

"In traditional practice, I often felt rushed and unable to address all of my patients' concerns," explains Dr. Katz. "Direct Primary Care removes those barriers. Now I can spend 30-60 minutes with each patient, be available by text or phone, and truly partner with families in their health journey."
